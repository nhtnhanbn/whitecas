cis(*θ*)

- *θ* is an angle

Returns cos(*θ*) + *i* sin(*θ*).

### Example

Find $$ \mathrm{cis} \left( -\dfrac{2\pi}{7} \right) + \mathrm{cis} \left( -\dfrac{12\pi}{7} \right) $$ in the form $$ A \cos(B \pi) $$. (2023 Specialist Exam 2 Section B Question 2fi)
