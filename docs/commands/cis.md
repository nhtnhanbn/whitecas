cis(*θ*)

- *θ* is an angle

Returns cos(*θ*) + *i* sin(*θ*).

### Example

Find $$ 4 \mathrm{cis} \left( -\dfrac{2\pi}{3} \right) $$ in cartesian form. (2018 Specialist Exam 2 Section A Question 4)
